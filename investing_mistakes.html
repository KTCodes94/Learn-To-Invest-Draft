Lesson 1: 8 Beginner Investing Mistakes to Avoid in the Stock Market

There is so much to know about the stock market. If you are anything like me, then you have little to no knowledge on how this all works.Lucky for us, I found a great YouTuber, Erika Kullberg, who breaks this all down. This lesson captures a few high level notes from the video below. Check it out!

Mistake #1

Not doing enough research

How To Avoid: Conduct research on the comapany you are interested including understanding their financial statements.

Mistake #2

Choosing the wrong strategy

How To Avoid: Understand the difference between long term and short term trading. Pick the one that suites you best.

Mistake #3

Paying too much in commission

How To Avoid: Know the commissions fees that are charged by brokerages and adjust accordingly.

Mistake #4

Not setting realistic expectations

How To Avoid: Invest in money that you won't be needing in the next 5 years. Understand that you are investing in the company's future not past or present.

Mistake #5

Not understanding the company first

How To Avoid: Do not act out of Fear of Missing Out (FOMO). Understand the company's business model.

Mistake #6

Falling in love with a company

How To Avoid: Acknowledge red flags and have an exit strategy prepared. Avoid investing too much money into one company.

Mistake #7

Buying based on an "expert's recommendation

How To Avoid: Understand that an "expert's" advice can be biased. Establish your own independent thought process.

Mistake #8

Not diversifying your portfolio

How To Avoid: Have several types of investments.
